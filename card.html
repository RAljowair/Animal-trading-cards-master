Falcon Peregrine

Its speed reaching over 320 km/h (200 mph) during its characteristic hunting stoop
(high-speed dive), making it the fastest bird in the world.

  • Scientific Name: Falco peregrinus
  • Average Length: 34cm-53cm
  • Average Lifespan: 13 years
  • Habitat: Around the world America, Middle East, japan, etc.

Usually bluish black to slate grey with indistinct darker barring the wingtips are black.The white to rusty underparts are barred with thin clean bands of dark brown or black.
